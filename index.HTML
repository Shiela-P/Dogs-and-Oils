About

When used properly essential oils can be a useful tool in your pet care arsenal. However, there are hundreds of oils on the market, and not all of them are pet safe. This can making selecting the right oils for your home and pets a daunting task. We are here to give you all the information needed to make educated purchases and keep your pets safe and happy!
